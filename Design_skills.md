Hero 1 Merlin
Health: 4.
Speed: 6.
Offensive skill: deal 4 damage to an enemy if the dice result is 6.
Passive skill: If you are the first to take action, you can choose to reroll the dice once.

Hero 2 Arthur 
Health: 7.
Speed: 4.
Offensive skill: deal 1 damage to an enemy if the dice result is 4 or more.
Passive skill: Record the dice result. Fully heal(reset the health attribute) a random hero if the result has been repeated.

Hero 3 Archer Chapanion
Health: 5.
Speed: 8.
Offensive skill: if you are the first to take action, deal 1 damage to an enemy and your speed gets -1(until a minimum of 3).
Passive skill: deal 1 damage to an enemy if the dice result is even.

Hero 4 Royal Architect
Health: 4.
Speed: 1.
Offensive skill: gives a friendly hero +2 defense(defense(buff): fully avoids the damage that does not exceed the number of defense. Set the defense to 0 after being attacked).
Passive skill: you can choose to deal the damage equal to the dice result to an enemy(could trigger only once in a battle).

Enemy example(the heroes are called as enemies from the opposite perspective):
Enemy 1 The rebel army
Health: 16.
Speed: 8.
Offensive skill: deal 1 damage to an enemy. If the dice result is 3 or less, deal 2 damage to another. 
Passive skill: 1 damage to an enemy and get -1 health(self) if the dice result is not 1.

Enemy 2 The Red Dragon
Health: 28.
Speed: 5.
Offensive skill: deal 2 damage to an enemy. If the dice result is 6, deal 2 damage to all instead. 
Passive skill: gets(self) +1 defense if the dice result is 3 or less.
