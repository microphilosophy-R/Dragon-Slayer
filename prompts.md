# Character Portrait Prompts

This document contains visual prompts for all characters in Dragon Slayer, categorized by type.

## Hero Portraits (Normal Characters)
*Style: 4:5 Aspect Ratio, Mythological Realism, Bust Portrait.*

### Merlin (merlin)
**Prompt:** An epic mythological oil painting focusing on Merlin's bust. A powerful wizard with an expressive, hyper-realistic face and deep-set eyes. He wears flowing robes of deep indigo silk embroidered with silver constellations. He is surrounded by swirling cosmic energy and shifting translucent distortions. Intense chiaroscuro lighting with brilliant highlights and deep, velvety shadows. Rendered in the style of 19th-century Mythological Realism. Rich, impasto brushwork and smooth, blended sfumato. 8k resolution, visible canvas grain. Vertical bust portrait, 4:5 aspect ratio (w-32 h-40), centered framing. Fullfill the canvas, do not leave blank spacing or add frames.
